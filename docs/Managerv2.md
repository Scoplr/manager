1. Smarter Task & Project Management

Dependencies & priorities: Let teams mark task relationships (Task A blocks Task B) without introducing complex Gantt charts.

Recurring tasks & templates: Automate repeatable workflows for onboarding, weekly reports, or checklists.

Notifications & reminders: Optional, intelligent nudges for overdue tasks or upcoming deadlines.

2. Knowledge & Onboarding Evolution

Rich content support: Markdown + file embedding, lightweight rich text, internal links.

Tagging & search: Make finding templates, documents, or processes intuitive.

Version history & rollback: Simple version control so teams can fix mistakes.

3. Dashboards & Insights

Visualizations: Task completion trends, bottlenecks, team load.

Custom views: Filter by project, priority, or owner.

Early warning signals: Highlight stalled tasks or overdue knowledge updates.

4. Team & Role Management

Lightweight roles: Admin, contributor, viewer, maybe “team lead.”

Permissions: Control what users can edit vs view.

Onboarding analytics: Track who’s active, who’s not completing tasks/templates.

5. Integrations / Portability

Calendar sync: Google/iCal for tasks and deadlines.

Slack / Teams notifications: Optional alerts for key updates.

Improved import/export: Full CSV/JSON for all entities; optional backups.

6. Personalization & “Delight” Features

Themes or color tokens: Let teams lightly brand their workspace.

Dashboard defaults: Users can choose compact vs expanded view.

Micro-interactions: Subtle animations, tooltips, drag-and-drop — all lightweight.

7. Analytics & Feedback Loop

Track engagement: which tasks/templates are used most, where friction occurs.

Measure adoption and retention at tier levels (under 20, 21–50, 51–100).

Use insights to iterate features and expand intelligently.
